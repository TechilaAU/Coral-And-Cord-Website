Returns & Exchanges

30 day returns on all full priced items. If the fit is not right or you have simply changed your mind, send it back unworn with tags attached and we will exchange it or refund you in full - whichever you prefer.

To start a return, email hello@coralandcord.com.au with your order number and we will send you a return label and instructions. Exchanges are posted back free of charge.

Limited edition Reef Collection releases are numbered runs, so exchanges are subject to availability - if your size is gone we will refund you instead.
